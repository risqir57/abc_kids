Abc Kids Business Day 2019

Business Day is an entrepreneurship event that’s held by ABC Kids Elementary School, which is able to teach students about entre- preneurship.

They learned how to deal and interact with people (sellers and buyers) around the school, either the buyers are from ABC Kids Elementary School or outside which makes them able to build confidence.

Beside improving their social skill, they learned algebra too. They need to count the amount of money, the change money, etc. Based on ABC Kids Elementary School’s principal, Ms. Monita, this year Business Day was held in 2 consecutive days as it deals with profit and the amount of students. There are around 400 students and less likely to be held in one day. In the first day, students from classes with letter a and b in the end (e.g. 1A, 1B, 2A, 2B…) shall be the seller, and students from classes with letter c and d in the end (e.g. 1C, 1D, 2C, 2D…) shall be the buyer. And on the next day, they exchanged their role, sellers became buyers and vise versa.

Of course there are some money circulated in an event. The amount of money circulated in the first day of Business Day were Rp 40,0 00,000,- and in the second day were Rp 50,0 00,000,-. Wow! As a buyer, this year’s Busin- ess Day was great, the products sold were good. Although some of the products prices were too expensive, they were still affordable. And as a seller, it was such a great experience. It’s such a big opportu nity to be able to learn how to promote and sell your products. Though for some, there weren’t many costumers that came, Business Day was great.
